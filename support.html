Frequently Asked
  • How do I add custom topics? Tap the “Topics” tab and choose + to add or remove interests.
  • Why am I not seeing full articles? Ensure “Full articles” is enabled in Settings — some sources may redirect externally.
  • How do I get breaking alerts? Go to Settings → Notifications and enable topics you want alerts for.
