AI Features

Newsphere uses AI to generate summaries, insights, and content recommendations based on the articles and topics you follow. The AI processes article text and user preferences to produce concise summaries and suggestions. We do not use AI to create content that impersonates human authors; instead, it assists with summarization and personalization.
